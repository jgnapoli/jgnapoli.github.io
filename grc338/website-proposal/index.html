Introduction

HONU Coffee Shop

HONU is a small coffee shop that started in my home town in the Santa Clarita Valley. There is only one store and it is a great way to relax with a quality cup of coffee.

Target audience

Young local high school and college students who always use this aesthetically pleasing cafe.

The students goals are to study and get homework done here since there is a large lounge area on the upper deck just above the baristas.
